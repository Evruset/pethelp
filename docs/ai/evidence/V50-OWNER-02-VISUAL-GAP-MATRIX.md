## Classification rules

- `VETO`: changes block order, primary action, responsive composition, state meaning, or owner-safe behavior.
- `MAJOR`: changes grouping, density, image/status treatment, or typography enough to alter comprehension.
- `MINOR`: native rasterization, scrollbars, antialiasing, or subpixel variance only.
- Prototype insurance, laboratory dynamics, reminder, and inferred clinical content are not implementation requirements until a bounded server-authoritative contract exists. Their omission cannot be filled with local data; the repair matches the surrounding hierarchy while preserving that product boundary.
